## Performance

**Grid 32x64:**
- Solve time: ~1 second
- GMRES iterations: <100 (typically converges quickly)

**Grid 48x96:**
- Solve time: ~1 minute (slow!)
- GMRES may struggle without preconditioner

**Recommendation:**
- Use nr=32, ntheta=64 for development
- Higher resolution needs ILU preconditioner (future work)

---

## Known Issues

1. **Slow for large grids** — nr>48 takes >1 min
2. **No preconditioner** — GMRES converges slowly
3. **Fixed BC type** — Only Dirichlet at outer boundary

**Future work (not in Phase 1):**
- Add ILU or multigrid preconditioner
- Support Neumann BC
- Optimize for large grids (parallel?)
