## Physics Gap Analysis

### v1.2 Solver Limitations

**Current Physics:**
```python
∂ψ/∂t = -η·J          # Resistive diffusion
∂ω/∂t = -ν·∇²ω        # Viscous diffusion
```

**Missing for Realistic Equilibria:**
- ❌ Poisson bracket [ψ, φ] (nonlinear advection)
- ❌ Pressure gradient ∇P
- ❌ Force balance J×B term
- ❌ Curvature terms (toroidal geometry)

**Consequence:**
- Works for **weak, approximately static** IC (cylindrical)
- Fails for **strong, dynamically balanced** IC (Solovev)
- **This is a physics limitation, not a bug**

---

### Why Solovev Fails

**Solovev Requirements:**
1. Force balance: J×B = ∇P
2. Maintained by nonlinear MHD coupling
3. Pressure gradient essential
4. Toroidal curvature effects

**v1.2 Behavior:**
1. Sees strong J
2. Applies resistive diffusion
3. Reduces J → breaks force balance
4. Plasma "explodes" (energy blows up)

**Fundamental Issue:** Solver doesn't know **why** equilibrium exists.

## v1.3 Requirements (Derived)

### Must-Have Physics

Based on v1.2.1 findings, v1.3 must include:

1. **Hamiltonian Structure**
   - Energy-conserving by design
   - Symplectic time integration
   - Target: < 1% energy drift intrinsic

2. **Poisson Bracket [ψ, φ]**
   - Nonlinear plasma advection
   - Couples flux and vorticity
   - Essential for equilibrium maintenance

3. **Pressure Gradient ∇P**
   - Force balance: J×B = ∇P
   - Required for realistic IC
   - Solovev needs this

4. **Curvature Terms**
   - Toroidal geometry effects
   - R-dependence in operators
   - Complete MHD physics

### Target Validation

**v1.3 Benchmark:**
- PyTokEq Solovev: energy drift < 1% (Tier 1) ✅
- Improvement over cylindrical baseline
- Demonstrate complete MHD physics

**Rationale:** v1.2.1 proved framework works, solver needs upgrade.
